Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2024  |  Last Updated: November 1, 2024

Krishi Portal ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our portal.

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device info, and approximate IP-based location — collected automatically for analytics.
  • Saved Preferences: Language, dark-mode, and market-alert settings stored locally on your device via localStorage.
  • Contact Information: Name, phone, and message text only if you submit our contact form voluntarily.

We do not collect Aadhaar numbers, bank account details, or any sensitive financial identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the portal and its features.
  • To personalise content such as local market prices and weather advisories.
  • To respond to enquiries submitted via our Contact page.
  • To analyse aggregate usage and improve the portal experience.
  • To comply with applicable Indian IT law.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to remember your preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. External links to government portals (Vikaspedia, IMD, ICAR) are governed by those sites' own policies.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share it only:

  • When required by government authorities or applicable law.
  • With trusted service providers operating under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When necessary to protect the safety or rights of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information. All contact form submissions use HTTPS. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Links

Our portal links to external resources (Vikaspedia, ICAR, IMD, Soil Health Portal). We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies separately.

7. Children's Privacy

Krishi Portal is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

Under the Information Technology Act, 2000 you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data we hold about you by contacting us through our Contact Us page.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the portal after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

  • Helpline: 1551 (Kisan Call Centre) — Toll-free, 06:00–22:00 IST
  • Online: Contact Us Page
