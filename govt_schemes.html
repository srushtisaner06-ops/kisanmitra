#001

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Financial support for crop loss/damage due to unforeseen events. Covers yields, post-harvest losses, and localized calamities.

info Eligibility: All farmers including sharecroppers/tenants in notified areas for notified crops.

#002

PM-KUSUM Scheme

Installation of solar pumps and solarization of existing grid-connected agriculture pumps to ensure energy security.

info Eligibility: Individual farmers, Water User Associations, and Primary Agriculture Credit Societies.
IRRIGATION RENEWABLE
#003

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Credit facility for cultivation requirements and ancillary needs like seeds, fertilizers, and pesticide procurement.

info Eligibility: Owner cultivators, tenant farmers, and sharecroppers involved in crop production.
